Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At Exam Venture, we believe your academic data is your business. This policy explains how our CGPA Calculator handles your information.

1. Data Collection & Storage

We do not collect, store, or share your personal academic data on any external servers. All information you enter—including course codes, units, and grades—is stored strictly on your device using Local Storage.

  • Your data never leaves your phone or computer.
  • If you clear your browser cache or delete the app, your data will be permanently removed.

2. Google AdSense & Cookies

To keep this tool free, we use Google AdSense to serve advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our sites.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

3. Third-Party Services

Apart from Google AdSense, we do not use any third-party tracking or analytics that monitor your behavior inside the app.

4. Your Consent

By using the Exam Venture CGPA Calculator, you consent to our storage of your data on your local device and the display of advertisements as described above.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, you can reach out to the Exam Venture development team through our official channels.
